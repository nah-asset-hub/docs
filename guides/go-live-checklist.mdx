## Production Access

Production access will be given after you have submitted all required document and pass KYC/KYB process from our end. Once the onboarding is approved, we will initiate the credentials sharing with the details shared in the preparation.

Expect 1 week SLA after document submission if there are no feedback for the document from provider/ partner.

## Credentials Sharing

To share credentials to your end, NAH will share the following information to you via admin/ first registered account on our end. 

- The credentials will need to be encrypted with NAH's public key and signed with Merchant's private key. 
- NAH will verify the signature of the credentials using merchant's shared public key

In order to do this, you will need to share your public key to NAH, and NAH will share NAH's public key to your side.

## Preparation Checklist

To share the information to NAH, please use your admin email and send the request to \<xxxxxx@xxx.com\>

1. Receive NAH Public Key & Configure it internally
2. Sharing your Public Key to NAH 
3. Receive Production API key credentials - Encrypted with your public key and signed with NAH private key
4. Sharing your Webhook URL to be configured in NAH side
5. Sharing your IP Address to be whitelisted to be configured in NAH side
6. Re-confirm your registered Settlement Accounts
7. Using Production API URL for production transaction
