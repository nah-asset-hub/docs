## How It Works

NAH work with Durianpay to provide full capability of on/off ramp. NAH with the help of CEX will be the one handling the digital asset/ crypto and Durianpay will be a payment service provider you need to connect to for fiat movement.

- **Key parties involved:**
  - Merchant
  - NAH: System provider for digital asset
  - Durianpay: PSP NAH works with to support fiat fund movement in Indonesia
  - CEX: Crypto Exchange you will be registered to

### Onboarding

- To use NAH service, Merchant need to be onboarded to both NAH & Durianpay.
  - Merchant can use NAH services after TnC is accepted/confirmed or signed by the merchant.
  - NAH will perform KYC/KYB against the merchant to use the service.
  - After the KYC/KYB from NAH is finished, NAH will be onboarding merchant to the CEX to use digital asset service.
  - Merchant can use NAH service once there are at least 1 CEX approve the merchant onboarding
