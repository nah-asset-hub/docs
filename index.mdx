## On-Off Ramp Overview

NAH enables **fast, compliant, and seamless stablecoin ↔ fiat conversion** between **USDT/USDC and Indonesian Rupiah (IDR)**.

We provide a \*\*conversion layer \*\*with help of the CEX and local PSP for fiat movement , allowing global partners to send or receive payments to/from Indonesia — without needing to manage their own exchange accounts or navigate local banking rails.

Our solution supports:

- **Off-Ramp (USDT → IDR)**
- **On-Ramp (IDR → USDT) (Coming Soon)**
- **Local pay outs** to suppliers, customers, and contractors in Indonesia via Durianpay
- We currently support the following digital asset
  - Supported Assets: USDT, USDC
  - Supported Assets Network: ERC20, POLYGON, TRC20, BEP20
  - Supported Fiat: IDR
  - Destination Country: Indonesia

## How It Works

NAH work with Durianpay to provide full capability of on/off ramp. NAH will be the one handling the digital asset/ crypto and Durianpay will be a payment service provider in which you need to connect to for fiat movement.

- **Key parties involved:**
  - Merchant
  - NAH: System provider for digital asset
  - Durianpay: PSP NAH works for to support fiat fund movement in Indonesia
  - CEX: Crypto Exchange you will be registered to

### Onboarding

- To use NAH service, Merchant need to be onboarded to both NAH & Durianpay.
- Merchant can use NAH services after TnC is accepted/confirmed or signed by the merchant.
- NAH will perform KYC/KYB against the merchant to use the service. After the KYC/KYB from NAH is finished, NAH will be onboarding merchant to the CEX to use digital asset service. Merchant can use NAH service once there are at least 1 CEX the merchant is onboarded into.
